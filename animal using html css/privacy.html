Privacy Policy

 
1. Information We Collect

    We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:
      a. Personal Information
        Name, email address, phone number, and address (for account registration, adoption, or volunteering).
        Payment details (for donations or adoption fees).
      b. Animal-Related Information
        Information about animals reported or registered, such as location, health status, and images.
      c. Technical Information
        IP address, browser type, operating system, and other technical details collected through cookies and similar technologies.
      d. User Feedback and Reports 
        Information provided through feedback forms or reporting stray animals.
        
2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information for the following purposes:
      To Provide Services:
       Manage animal profiles, adoption processes, healthcare tracking, and donation records.
      To Improve User Experience:
       Personalize your interactions with our platform and enhance functionality.
      To Facilitate Payments:
       Process donations, sponsorships, and adoption fees securely.
      To Communicate:
       Send notifications, updates, and responses to inquiries or reports.
      To Ensure Security:
      Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and misuse of our services.
      
3. How We Share Your Information

    We may share your information under the following circumstances:
      With Volunteers and Partners:
       To facilitate animal care, adoption, or rescue operations.
      With Payment Processors:
       To securely handle transactions.
      As Required by Law:
       To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.
      With Your Consent:
       When you explicitly agree to share your information for specific purposes.
       
4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    We use cookies and similar technologies to:
      Remember your preferences.
       Track website usage for analytics and performance improvement.
       Enhance the functionality of interactive features like maps and donation systems.
       You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.
       
5. Data Security

     We take appropriate measures to protect your information, including:
     Encryption of sensitive data (e.g., payment details).
